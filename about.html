About

 

Background

My name is Aaron, and I am a designer living and working in San Francisco. Born and raised in the Silicon Valley, I grew up questioning every product and interface I interacted with, leading me to study a mix of computer science and product design at Stanford University.

In the past, I have worked as both a software engineer and designer, sometimes in hybrid roles. Previous experience includes larger companies (IDEO, Apple) and smaller startups (Qualia, Nextdoor) alike. In 2015, I was also a KPCB Engineering Fellow.

Approach

I am a generalist, able to mock up user flows and prototype them in code. I've also worked in a wide variety of disciplines: user research, UI/UX design, prototyping, front-end web development, and even industrial design. I strongly believe in user-centered design, and I will always advocate the best experience for the user given the constraints.
