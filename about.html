Born and raised in the Silicon Valley, I've always been engaged in the culture of innovation, constantly asking how the world around me could be better. In college I pursued this predisposition by studying a combination of computer science and product design.

I'm a generalist and full-stack designer, able to mock up user flows and prototype in code. I prefer a human-centered design process steeped in user research, rapid iteration, and data-driven decisions. And while my focus is on digital products and experiences, I've spent time in the machine shop making a few physical products as well.

In recent years, I've worked at IDEO, Nextdoor, and Apple, doing anything and everything from interaction design to software engineering. I was also a Kleiner Perkins Caufield & Byers Engineering Fellow in 2015. If you're wondering what I do in my free time, I like to ride my bike and take photos, sometimes simultaneously.

Feel free to shoot me an email at aaronrossbrown@gmail.com if you'd like to discuss a possible project or venture, or if you'd just like to get in touch.
