Bike Bench

Spring 2015 · Stanford, CA

At Stanford, ME 203 is a rite of passage for mechanical engineering and product design students. In the class, students manufacture a product of their own design using at least one of three main processes in Stanford's Product Realization Lab: machining, sand casting, or welding.

I chose to do a welding project, building a bench that could seat both people and bikes. Designing the bench
