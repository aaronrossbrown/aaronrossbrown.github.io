Creative Coding Intern · Fall 2015 · New York, NY

In the fall of 2015, I lived and worked in NYC as part of the first run of the Stanford in New York program. The program involves immersive studies in the city of New York focused on the arts, architecture, urban studies, and design. I was lucky to be selected to the first cohort of students in its inaugural quarter.

Aside from taking classes as a full-time student, I also worked four days a week at IDEO's NYC studio. As a creative coding intern, I worked on a variety of tasks, from user research to interaction design to prototyping concepts. Although I can't show my work on client projects, I'm more than willing to talk about my experience and the things I learned.
