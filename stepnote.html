Work About Resume

Stepnote

Winter 2015 · Stanford, CA

Overview

Stepnote is a song annotation web app for choreographers to listen to music and note ideas for possible steps and movements.

Role

Created prototypes for user testing. Contributed on design and development efforts.

Scope

4 weeks

Teammates

Ishita Prasad, Tulsee Doshi

Categories

Needfinding, Prototyping, User testing, UX design, Front-end development

Approach

Testing our Hypotheses

Moving forward, we decided to stick with our best idea, Song Annotator—the idea was feasible to prototype and test in three weeks, and it seemed to resonate best with the choreographers we interviewed. Now there remained two questions to answer. What screen size would we develop for, web or mobile? And what format of annotations would we support: text, audio, or video?

We quickly moved to low-fidelity paper prototypes, testing the possible combinations of screen size and annotation format with multiple choreographers. Before, long, we had chosen a web application that would support text based annotations.
