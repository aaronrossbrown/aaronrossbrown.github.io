Consistency at Nextdoor

Nextdoor is a startup building the social private network for neighborhoods. Their product is a platform of web and mobile apps through which neighbors are able to connect, share news and events, buy and sell items, recommend local businesses, and more.

As part of the 2015 cohort of KPCB Fellows, I joined Nextdoor and worked on a number of engineering and design projects for them. One of these projects was a consistency audit of web elements and assets, through which I created a design toolkit for use across the company.
