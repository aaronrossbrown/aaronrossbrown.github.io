Redesigning the Wallboard

During my time at IDEO, I worked within a team of designers to help redesign Wilsonart's sample wallboard, which lives in the workspaces of interior designers and architects worldwide. These wallboards act as salesmen for Wilsonart and are used to compare and choose laminates from Wilsonart and its competitors.

While I can't share further details, feel free to read more about the project and its outcomes in the press release and Medium article.
